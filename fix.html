​​International Business Travel

International business trave​l includes any company-authorized business activity outside the continental United States and Canada for which Westat is responsible for all travel, lodging, and per diem expenditures. This includes international connections and layovers.

Westat employees are encouraged to make travel arrangements through the Westat Travel Office. For those traveling internationally, Westat's Security Services can provide a range of support services designed to enhance your personal safety and security while traveling. If you are part of a group of five or more employees traveling on the same international flight, contact Security Services at least 30 days in advance of the trip.

Support Before International Business Travel

  • Staff are encouraged to make travel arrangements through Westat Travel
  • Passport must be up-to-date
  • Contact Security Services(email link) by email at least 10 days before the date of your departure
    • Provide your name, destination, dates of travel, flight itinerary and lodging information
    • Security Services will arrange a mutually convenient meeting date and time

International Business Travel Personal Safety Briefing

Security Services can provide a country-specific preparatory briefing with travel-safety information customized to your international destination. These briefings are based upon real-time information from Westat's international travel support vendor that specializes in travel security and threat intelligence. The briefing includes:

Destination specific safety and security

  • United States State Department Travel Warnings
  • International Destination Overview
  • Executive summary
  • Country and city-specific facts, currency form, and electronic communication methods
  • Current political and economic analysis
  • Review of real and potential safety and security concerns and prevention strategies
  • Review of recent safety or security events to determine if travel is warranted at present
  • Customs / Immigration information
  • Cultural information and any potential adverse impacts on Westat travelers
  • Significant national dates, i.e., holidays, elections, celebrations
  • Diplomatic issues
  • Local public safety contact information
  • Airport and ground transportation information
  • Accepted methods of payment for goods and services

Medical and Hospital Information

  • Executive summary
    • Overall medical and health risk rating
    • Real and potential diseases and health risks
    • Bed bug information
  • Local health care options
  • Quality of care
  • Languages offered and proficiency
  • Food and Water Consumption
  • Pharmacy – Access to Medicine
  • Accepted methods of payment for services and medicine
  • Recommended immunizations and vaccinations
  • Recommended prevention options
  • Food and potable water safety
