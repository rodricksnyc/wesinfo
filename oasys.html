OAsys User Services

OAsys provides support for a wide range of Information Technology (IT) needs. Hardware and software assistance and solutions are provided by the PC HelpDesk, a "one-stop-shop" for all types of problem solving.

Information about Westat's standard desktop software (email, word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, and simple database use) is available by attending the monthly classes offered by OAsys. In addition, regular and special use software documentation is developed and published in WesInfo, under the OAsys Training and Documentation section as Quick Starts, Fact Sheets, and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs).

Collaboration needs for campus staff, satellite offices, remote staff, and clients are met through the development and use of SharePoint services. Westat's distributed workforce is brought together in small and large groups for trainings, meetings, and focus groups with Webex video/audio conferencing services.

Corporate email support and services are provided by the OAsys unit. The unit also provides configuration and delivery of standard desktop software, operating systems, and security patches for Westat computing systems.

The OAsys unit provides a large loaner equipment pool (laptops, projectors, hand-held devices, and more) for presentations, conferences, and out-of-office needs. Loaner equipment set ups can be requested for meetings held in campus conference rooms. Technical support for the RW5 Conference Center is provided by the Loaner Services staff as well.

OAsys is located(page in PDF format) on the third floor of the RA building in Room 1374F. Contact OAsys by calling the PC HelpDesk at (301) 251-4292 or sending email to PC Help(email link).
